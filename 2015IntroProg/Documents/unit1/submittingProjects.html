Submitting Projects and Labs

Here are steps

Note:  01/2012 - Some of the screen prints below are no longer current.   For guidance in submitting Learning Units this semester, please see the "Getting Started..." booklet.

  • Delete the existing unitX.zip file if it exists (where 'X' stands for the Learning Unit number.)

  • Compress your unitX directory into a new unitX.zip file. It will be unit1.zip the first time we do this.

  • Login to Blackboard

  • Click on the course link
  • Click on the Project And Lab Submission link

  • Click on ">> View/Complete Assignment:..."

  • Click the Browse button and find your unitX.zip file and attach it

  • Enter your name in the Comments area.

  • Press the Submit button, not the Save button.

  • Done!
