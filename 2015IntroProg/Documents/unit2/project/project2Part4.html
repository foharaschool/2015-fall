Problem Statement

Write a program that calculates several possible tips to give to a waiter at a restaurant. Ask the user to enter the original cost of the meal and then calculate a tip at 10%, 12.5%, 15%, 17.5%, and 20%. Write the original cost of the meal to the page. Write all the possible tips and the new final cost to the page.  Note that a meal cost may have a dollars and cents value, i.e. do not assume that only whole numbers will be entered by the user.
