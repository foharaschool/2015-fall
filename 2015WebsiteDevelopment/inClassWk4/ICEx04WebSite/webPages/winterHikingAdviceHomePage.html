Advice for Winter Hiking

Clothing Advice:

Dress Warmly. Be sure to wear warm covering on your head, and cover your face in the event that it is windy.

Wear Snowshoes for Deep Snow. It is very tiring to wade through deep snow. If the snow is deep, wear snow shoes.

Wildlife Advice:

Don't Pet Nor Feed the Bears. If you encounter any bears, do not attempt to feed or pet them. They may be grouchy from having not eaten for awhile.

Emergency Advice:

Carry Snack Food. Bring along some snack food in case you get really hungry.

Carry a Cell Phone. Bring a cell phone along in case your party gets lost or has a member who suffers a serious injury.

Learning Advice:

Carry proper equipment Bring along a camera rated for cold conditions to record observations.

Carry guidebooks Bring along any guide books to help understand your observations.
