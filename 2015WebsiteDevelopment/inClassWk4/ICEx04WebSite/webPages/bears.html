Bears in Wisconsin

Range:

Bears are not supposed to be in southern Wisconsin. However, in recent years, there are more and more reports of bears turning up from time to time in the southern part of the state.

Danger:

Unless it is a Grizzly bear, it is mostly vegetarian. Thus, you will not be considered a source of food for the bear. Just look it right in the eye to let it know you are not afraid, and then back slowly away.

In the spring, if a bear has a cub nearby, it will attack to protect the cub. Never approach a bear cub, no matter how cute.
